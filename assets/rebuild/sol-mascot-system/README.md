## Direction decision

The public story is about recognizable mascot direction, not a completed reusable system. Sol’s fennec muzzle, oversized ears, amber eyes, exactly three forehead marks, one banded tail, compact animal paws, ivory-and-terracotta costume, leather structure, bronze accents, and leaf-topped staff family form the identity lock. The perspective sheet exposes orientation and rear-silhouette questions; the greeting, guide, delighted seated, and thoughtful targets expose gesture and expression requirements for a future matching model and rig.
