## Direction decision

The public story is about visible material hierarchy, not a completed PBR asset. Deep-brown skin and tightly coiled hair anchor the face; teal cloth and ivory linen carry the largest soft masses; worn leather organizes the torso, waist, forearms, and boots; oxidized bronze creates harder accents; carved wood, amber glass, and stone complete the staff, lantern, and base. The inspection sheet compares coordinated color, warm-gray form, and close-detail presentation rather than one traceable production asset.
