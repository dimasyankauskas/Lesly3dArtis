Review Language

Clear language for different audiences

Client View

"This shows the intended mood, scale, finish direction, and presence of the bar cabinet before final fabrication details are locked."

  • Approve the overall visual direction.
  • Review the proposed blackened steel, dark wood, warm glass, and stone direction.
  • Confirm whether the visual direction matches the quality and setting of the space.

Studio View

"This review layer makes the commission easier to discuss before final detailing."

  • Identify what still needs material or hardware confirmation.
  • Keep visual revisions separate from fabrication revisions.
  • Create reusable presentation assets for future clients.

Fabrication View

"This does not replace shop drawings. It supports them by keeping presentation language separate from build documentation."

  • Use final CAD/PDF dimensions before fabrication-accurate renders.
  • Label concept visuals separately from production visuals.
  • Keep exact drawings as the source of truth for build details.
