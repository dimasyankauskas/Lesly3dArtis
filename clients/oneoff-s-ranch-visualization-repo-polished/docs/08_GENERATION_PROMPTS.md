# 08 — Image Generation Prompts

See `prompts/image/` for copy-ready prompts.

## Master constraints
Always include:

- four lower panels total
- two panels per side
- sliding bifold mechanism
- shallow-to-moderate recessed niche behind, not a room
- upper arched glass area from the drawing
- warm wood millwork
- blackened steel / dark bronze frames
- dark stone countertop
- side shelves

## Negative constraints
Always include:

- no four independent swing doors
- no full kitchen or room behind the opening
- no extra panels
- no white aluminum frames
- no modern patio door look
- no missing right closed pair when only left is open
- no panels blocking side shelves unless that is confirmed by the mechanism
- no final lattice, stained-glass pattern, or exact color claim unless One Off confirms it

## Recommendation
Generate one asset at a time and QA it against `docs/03_VISUAL_APPROACH_AND_QC.md`.
