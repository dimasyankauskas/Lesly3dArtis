# 09 — Video Prompt Strategy

## Best video type
For Gemini/Veo or similar image-to-video tools, use subtle camera movement rather than complex object animation first.

## Recommended video sequence
1. Closed hero: slow left-to-right inspection.
2. Left-pair-open detail: slight push-in toward the folded pair and shallow niche.
3. Both-sides-open: slow inspection of clear center opening.
4. Mechanism explainer: simple animated diagram, not photoreal, showing fold and slide states.

## Important instruction
The furniture and mechanism should not morph. If rendering a video from a still image, ask for camera-only motion unless generating a dedicated mechanism animation.

See `prompts/video/gemini_veo_inspection_video.md` and `prompts/video/mechanism_animation_prompt.md`.
