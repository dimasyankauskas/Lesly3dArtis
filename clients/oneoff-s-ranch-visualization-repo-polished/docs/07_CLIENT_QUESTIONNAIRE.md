# 07 — Questions for One Off / Project Intake

## Technical clarification
1. Are the lower four panels confirmed as two sliding bifold pairs?
2. When only the left side is open, should exactly two panels fold/park left while the right two stay closed?
3. When both sides are open, where exactly do the folded pairs park?
4. Is the rail at both top and bottom confirmed?
5. Is the rail intended to be hidden inside square tube?
6. What is the intended clear opening width when both sides are open?
7. Is the wall/cove depth approximately 12 inches, as the client-message intake indicates, or has that changed?
8. Is the area behind the opening a shallow bar/display niche or a deeper back-bar area?
9. Are the dimensions in the coordination drawing still reference-only?
10. Do you want visual accuracy to match the drawing exactly or communicate final intended look after fabrication changes?

## Material / finish
1. Metal finish: blackened steel, bronze, painted black, raw steel, or other?
2. Wood species / finish direction?
3. Stone countertop type / color?
4. Are specific colors confirmed yet? Current intake says not yet.
5. Is the lattice / stained-glass infill still undetermined, and should it be left out for the first visual pass?
6. Interior shelf finish and lighting temperature?

## Client presentation
1. Is this visual for internal review, designer review, or end-client approval?
2. Does the client need to approve movement/function or only overall appearance?
3. Do you want a private client page or just images/PDF?
4. Should the visuals show one state or multiple states?
5. Do you want progress updates after fabrication begins?

## Business workflow
1. How often do clients ask for 3D or visual clarification?
2. What usually causes approval delays?
3. Which projects would benefit from a private commission portal?
4. Would One Off want reusable visual templates for future projects?
5. Who inside One Off should receive client comments/approvals?
