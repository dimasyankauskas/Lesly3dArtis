# Skill: Bar Window Technical Review

Use this skill before writing prompts, visual QA, or technical explanations for the S-RANCH bar-window.

## Mechanism
Four lower panels total. Two sliding bifold pairs. Left pair folds/parks left. Right pair folds/parks right.

## Depth
Behind opening is a shallow-to-moderate recessed bar/display niche, not a full room.

Current client-message evidence says the wall/cove is understood as approximately 12 inches thick. Treat 12 inches as the working assumption unless One Off confirms otherwise.

## Hardware / fabrication notes

- Windows slide on a linear rail.
- Shoulder bolt anchors the window to a bearing.
- Linear rail is planned at top and bottom.
- Rail may be buried/concealed inside square tube.
- Four tall windows are planned as 3/4 x 1 1/2 tube.
- Dimensions are reference/coordination; One Off will define the final look.

## Finish / infill boundary

- Specific colors are not confirmed.
- Lattice / stained-glass infill is not determined and can be left out for now.
- Do not force lattice, diamond glass, walnut, bronze, blackened steel, or exact final finishes unless One Off confirms them.
- It is acceptable to use warm high-end material mood as concept direction if labeled as concept.

## Red flags
- four independent swing doors
- four panels folded to one side
- full kitchen / room behind opening
- missing upper arched glass area
- overclaiming final colors or lattice/infill
- white aluminum patio-door style
