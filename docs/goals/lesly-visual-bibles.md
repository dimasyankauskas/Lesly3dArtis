# Lesly Visual Bibles And Reconstruction Targets

These bibles lock project identity before any replacement image is generated. They describe public art direction and Lesly's future 3D reconstruction targets; they are not evidence of completed models, topology, UVs, rigs, or exports.

## Shared Presentation System

- Style: premium stylized realism with readable primary forms, controlled tertiary detail, and believable material response.
- Tone: cinematic dark studio, terracotta and aged bronze accents, warm ivory highlights, restrained environment support.
- Beauty lighting: warm key, soft neutral fill, narrow rim, readable face/hands/materials, grounded contact shadow.
- Inspection lighting: neutral three-point studio lighting, plain charcoal background, no dramatic shadow hiding anatomy or construction.
- Background: near-black brown/charcoal gradient; no unrelated scenery, floating decoration, generated text, logos, watermarks, or software UI.
- Anatomy: adult proportions, structurally credible joints, five fingers and toes where visible, natural grip/contact, no fused accessories or ambiguous limbs.
- Output rule: generate one clean view at a time. Captions, labels, process explanations, arrows, and evidence status remain HTML.

## Flagship Warrior — Desert Guardian

Bible version: 1.1. The weapon hand was clarified after the first portrait review: right-hand dominance gives the strongest readable silhouette while the left-only pauldron remains the costume asymmetry anchor. This is an art-direction refinement, not a production-evidence claim.

### Role And Read

An adult desert guardian designed for stylized fantasy game direction. She should read in three seconds through a long segmented braid, asymmetric ivory wrap top, terracotta layered cloth, aged-bronze armor, dark fitted leg layers, and a crescent-bladed polearm.

### Locked Identity

- Adult woman, approximately late twenties to early thirties; athletic and capable, not doll-like or adolescent.
- Warm medium-brown skin with natural variation.
- Oval face, defined high cheekbones, straight medium-width nose, almond dark-brown eyes, strong brows, and full natural lips.
- Calm, alert expression; gaze slightly off-camera for beauty views and straight ahead for neutral views.
- Thick dark-brown hair gathered into one long segmented braid with loose controlled strands at the temples. Never switch to a high ponytail, short braid, or unbound hair within this series.
- Approximately 7.5-head stylized-realistic proportion; strong shoulders and legs; balanced hips; practical stance.

### Locked Costume Construction

1. Ivory linen asymmetric wrap top crossing the torso and anchoring beneath the opposite arm; secure, physically constructible, and consistent in every view.
2. Single aged-bronze shoulder pauldron on her left shoulder with leather under-straps; matching bronze forearm guards.
3. Wide layered brown-leather waist belt with one central circular sun/compass buckle.
4. Terracotta front and back cloth panels plus one longer split cape/skirt layer; restrained woven border pattern, consistent hem and attachment points.
5. Dark charcoal fitted trousers/leggings beneath the cloth layers.
6. Brown leather calf wraps and open-toe reinforced sandals/boots with consistent strap count and direction.
7. Two large aged-bronze hoop earrings; no random jewelry changes.

### Locked Weapon And Props

- One long dark-wood polearm with aged-bronze crescent blade and geometric guard.
- Weapon length approximately 1.35 times character height.
- Right hand holds the shaft near the balance point with a complete natural grip; blade points down and outward without intersecting the stage.
- Left hand remains relaxed or supports the shaft only in explicitly planned alternate views.
- Beauty stage: low circular carved-stone plinth, one restrained pottery urn behind the rear-right silhouette, and at most two small rock clusters.

### Locked Materials

- Skin: warm natural satin response, no plastic sheen.
- Ivory linen: visible woven softness, high roughness.
- Terracotta cloth: deeper red-orange, medium-high roughness, subtle woven border.
- Leather: dark brown with edge wear only at plausible contact points.
- Bronze: aged warm bronze, controlled highlights, darker recess patina.
- Hair: dark brown with broad grouped strands before flyaway detail.
- Stone: matte dark sandstone with restrained carved geometry.

### Required Clean Source Views

1. Desktop hero, 16:9, full head and body, mobile-safe central silhouette.
2. Portrait/mobile hero, 4:5, head through complete feet and weapon grip visible.
3. Neutral front reconstruction target.
4. Neutral left side reconstruction target.
5. Neutral back reconstruction target.
6. Face and upper-costume close-up.
7. Natural weapon-grip and hand close-up.
8. Boots, feet, cloth fall, and stage-contact close-up.
9. Costume front/back direction pair using the same identity and construction.
10. Material-response close-ups for linen, terracotta cloth, leather, bronze, hair, and skin.

### Rejection Rules

- Reject ponytail, cropped hair, different braid, different face, different skin tone, sword, staff, orb, extra weapon, or mirrored pauldron.
- Reject exposed/changed costume construction, inconsistent belt/buckle, missing back cloth, fused fingers, floating weapon, hidden feet, excessive cleavage, or pin-up posing.
- Reject any generated wireframe, UV layout, software screenshot, topology overlay, or export label.
