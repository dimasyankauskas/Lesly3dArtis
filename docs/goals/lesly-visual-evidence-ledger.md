## Evidence Vocabulary

- `KEEP`: current asset may remain after responsive and trademark checks.
- `REBUILD`: replace with a consistent, professionally reviewed source visual.
- `HOLD_FOR_REAL_CAPTURE`: project remains public, but this proof role cannot be filled until Lesly supplies a genuine capture or source file.
- `BLOCK`: current asset cannot serve its present public role because it is misleading, internally inconsistent, or technically meaningless.

Public provenance is one of `GENERATED_DIRECTION`, `VERIFIED_CAPTURE`, `PROJECT_EVIDENCE`, `CONTEXT_ILLUSTRATION`, or `HOLD_FOR_REAL_CAPTURE`.

## Promotion Checklist

For every replacement record:

1. Source/provenance classification.
2. Approved identity/style bible version.
3. Intended public role and caption.
4. Full-resolution anatomy, construction, and artifact inspection.
5. Desktop and mobile crop proof.
6. File dimensions, sRGB, format, and size budget.
7. Independent reviewer status and score.
8. Genuine evidence link for every technical claim, or `HOLD_FOR_REAL_CAPTURE`.
