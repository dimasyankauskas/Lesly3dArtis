# Platform Strategy — Lesly 3D Artist

## Executive recommendation

Lesly should not position herself as a generic “AI artist” or broad “3D designer.” The stronger position is:

> 3D Character Artist & AI-Assisted Visual Designer creating production-ready characters, avatars, mascots, and visual assets for games, creators, startups, and brands.

The flagship offer should be:

> I turn sketches, AI references, moodboards, and rough ideas into polished, production-ready 3D characters.

This uses AI as a workflow advantage while still selling human skill: taste, character design, 3D execution, consistency, and usable deliverables.

---

## Best initial platform stack

### Upwork

Use for active freelance demand capture.

Recommended positioning:

- Game-ready 3D character artist
- Character concept to 3D production
- Stylized 3D characters for indie games
- Mascots / avatars / creature models

### Contra

Use as a clean professional storefront with packaged services.

Recommended packages:

- Concept to 3D Character
- Game-Ready Character Package
- Brand Mascot System
- Avatar / Creator Character
- Art Direction Consultation

### ArtStation

Use for credibility with game, film, concept, and 3D clients.

What to show:

- Final renders
- Turnarounds
- Sculpt details
- Wireframes/topology
- Texture/material sheets
- Case-study breakdowns

### Behance

Use for visual case studies and broader creative-client discovery.

Best project format:

- Problem
- Direction
- Character exploration
- 3D process
- Final renders
- Deliverables
- Use case

### LinkedIn

Use for direct outreach, founder/agency visibility, and trust-building.

Content themes:

- Before/after: AI reference → final 3D character
- Behind-the-scenes process
- Short case studies
- Service posts for startups, game teams, and creators
- Educational posts about what makes a 3D character production-ready

### Fiverr / project catalogs

Use only for narrow, productized offers.

Avoid broad gigs like “I will make any 3D model.” Use buyer-intent offers:

- I will turn your concept art into a stylized 3D character
- I will create a brand mascot character for your startup
- I will create a game-ready 3D character asset
- I will model a stylized Roblox / avatar accessory

---

## Strongest service niches

### 1. Concept / AI Reference → 3D Character

Many clients can generate concepts now, but they cannot make them coherent, usable, rig-ready, game-ready, or brand-consistent.

Ideal clients:

- Game founders
- Indie studios
- AI product teams
- Creators
- Agencies
- Startups

### 2. Game-Ready Stylized Characters

Deliverables:

- Sculpt
- Retopo
- UVs
- Textures
- FBX / OBJ / BLEND
- Unity/Unreal-ready export notes

### 3. Brand Mascots for Startups and AI Products

Deliverables:

- Mascot concept
- 3D model
- Poses
- Expression set
- Social/media render pack
- Brand style notes

### 4. Avatar / VTuber / Streamer Character

Deliverables:

- Avatar concept
- 3D model
- Outfit/accessory variations
- Expression set
- Pose renders
- Optional rig-ready preparation

### 5. Roblox / UGC / Stylized Assets

Deliverables:

- Accessories
- Clothing
- Props
- Stylized asset sets
- Platform-aware optimization

---

## What to avoid

Avoid leading with:

- Generic AI art
- Generic 3D modeling
- Logo design
- Simple portrait commissions
- NFT-heavy language
- “I can make anything” positioning
- Too many unrelated art styles in one place

The website and profiles should make Lesly look like a specialist who understands character production, not a generalist experimenting with tools.
