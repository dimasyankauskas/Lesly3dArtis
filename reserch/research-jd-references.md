# Research References — 10 JD Sample and Extracted Terms

## Purpose

This research note compiles the JD/title/keyword signals used to rebuild Lesly’s website and LinkedIn materials. The goal was to avoid invented titles and use professional terms appearing in current/recent 3D-character-related job descriptions or freelance project excerpts.

## Main correction

**Use:** 3D Character Artist

“AI-Assisted Visual Designer” was not found as a role title in this 10-JD sample. AI appears only as a workflow/input context in some project descriptions, such as “AI references” or “AI-generated base models.”
