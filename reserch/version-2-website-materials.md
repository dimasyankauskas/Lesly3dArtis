# 8. Process Section

## Section headline

**A clear process from brief to delivery**

## Step 1 — Brief

We define the character, use case, style, references, technical requirements, timeline, budget, and final deliverables.

## Step 2 — Visual Direction

Lesly refines the character direction using sketches, moodboards, references, and optional AI-assisted exploration when appropriate.

## Step 3 — 3D Creation

The character is built through sculpting, modeling, topology, UVs, textures, materials, and presentation.

## Step 4 — Review & Polish

You review key stages so silhouette, personality, proportions, materials, and final details can be refined before delivery.

## Step 5 — Delivery

You receive final renders and agreed production files such as BLEND, FBX, OBJ, texture maps, or other formats depending on the project.

# 11. FAQ Copy

## Do I need a finished concept before contacting Lesly?

No. Lesly can work from sketches, moodboards, written descriptions, AI references, existing characters, or rough ideas.

## Can Lesly turn an AI-generated image into a 3D character?

Yes, when the image can be used legally and ethically. AI references can help communicate direction, but the final character is refined through human art direction and 3D production work.

## Can Lesly create game-ready characters?

Yes. Depending on project scope, deliverables may include optimized topology, UVs, textures, source files, export files, and real-time handoff notes.

## What file formats can be delivered?

Depending on scope, deliverables may include BLEND, FBX, OBJ, PNG texture maps, final renders, and other agreed formats.

## Does Lesly offer rigging or animation?

Basic rig-ready preparation may be included depending on scope. Full rigging or animation can be quoted separately or handled with a specialist if needed.

## How much does a 3D character cost?

Pricing depends on complexity, style, detail level, technical requirements, timeline, and deliverables. A simple stylized asset costs less than a complete game-ready character with topology, UVs, textures, and export files.

## How long does a project take?

Small assets may take days. Complete characters, mascots, or game-ready assets may take several weeks depending on scope and review cycles.

## Can Lesly work remotely?

Yes. Projects can be handled remotely using clear briefs, references, staged reviews, and structured delivery.
