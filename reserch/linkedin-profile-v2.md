- Clean topology and optimization
- PBR or stylized texturing
- Rigging, skinning, deformation, or animation-prep awareness when applicable

- Strong portfolio examples showing process, not only final renders
- Collaboration with concept artists, animators, technical artists, designers, engineers, founders, or creative leads
- AI as an optional workflow accelerator, not as the final product
