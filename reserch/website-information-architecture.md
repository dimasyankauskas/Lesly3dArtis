# Website Information Architecture — Lesly 3D Artist

The website should convert visitors into paid project inquiries by showing:

- What Lesly creates
- Who she helps
- What problem she solves
- What the client receives
- Why she is different
- How to start a project quickly

> I help game teams, creators, startups, and brands turn ideas into polished 3D characters, avatars, mascots, and production-ready visual assets.

## Homepage structure

### 1. Hero

**Goal:** Explain what Lesly does within 5–10 seconds.

**Display:**

- Hero character render or Lesly portrait + character artwork
- Headline
- Short value proposition
- Primary and secondary CTAs
- Specialty tags

**Example headline:**

> Custom 3D Characters, Avatars & Mascots for Games, Creators, and Brands

**Example subheadline:**

> I turn sketches, AI concepts, references, and rough ideas into polished 3D characters ready for games, animation, streaming, product worlds, and brand storytelling.

### 2. Problem / opportunity

Show that Lesly understands the client’s pain:

- I have a concept, but I need it turned into 3D.
- My AI image looks cool, but it is not production-ready.
- I need a character for my game, brand, pitch, or creator identity.
- I need someone who understands art direction and technical delivery.

### 3. Services overview

Service cards:

1. Concept-to-3D Character
2. Game-Ready Character Package
3. Avatar / VTuber / Streamer Character Design
4. Brand Mascot / Product Character
5. Roblox / UGC / Stylized Asset Design
6. AI-Assisted Visual Development
7. Art Direction & Character Pipeline Consulting

### 4. Featured work

Use 6–9 curated pieces, not a huge gallery.

Recommended filters:

- Game Characters
- Avatars
- Mascots
- Creatures
- Roblox / UGC
- AI-to-3D
- Stylized
- Realistic
- Process Breakdowns

### 5. Process

Use a 5-step process:

1. Brief
2. Visual Direction
3. 3D Creation
4. Review & Polish
5. Delivery

### 6. Packages / pricing structure

Suggested package categories:

- Character Concept Sprint
- 3D Character Starter
- Game-Ready Character
- Brand Mascot System
- Creative / Pipeline Consulting

### 7. Who this is for

Ideal client cards:

- Indie Game Teams
- Startup Founders
- Streamers & Creators
- Roblox / UGC Creators
- Agencies
- AI Product Teams

### 8. Why work with Lesly

Differentiators:

- Artistic foundation
- 3D production skill
- AI-enhanced workflow
- Character-first thinking
- Flexible for different client types

### 9. Before / after showcase

High-converting examples:

- Sketch → 3D Character
- AI Reference → Production-Ready 3D
- Moodboard → Mascot
- Sculpt → Retopo → Texture → Render

### 10. Start a Project CTA

**CTA headline:**

> Have a character, mascot, or avatar idea?

**CTA copy:**

> Send your brief, sketch, AI reference, or moodboard and I’ll recommend the best production path.
