# Chat History — Lesly 3D Artist Website Project

This is a practical project transcript and decision log. It summarizes the useful conversation history without private internal reasoning.

## 1. Market and platform strategy

**Initial goal:** Find where Lesly, an artist, painter, 3D character designer, and AI-tool expert, can promote herself for remote/freelance work and start taking orders quickly.

**Recommendation:** Do not position her as a generic AI artist or generic 3D designer. Position her as a production-ready character artist who turns sketches, AI references, rough concepts, and brand ideas into usable 3D characters/assets.

**Recommended platform stack:**

- Fast order capture: Upwork, Fiverr, Contra, VGen-style creator commission platforms where rules allow
- Credibility: ArtStation, Behance, LinkedIn
- Game-specific opportunities: ArtStation Jobs, Polycount, Work With Indies, InGameJob, Hitmarker
- Later passive income: Fab, Sketchfab Store, Gumroad

**Flagship service:**

> Concept / AI Reference → Production-Ready 3D Character
